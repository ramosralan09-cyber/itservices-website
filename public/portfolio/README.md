## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Recommended size**: 1200x800px or higher
- **Aspect ratio**: 3:2 (landscape) preferred
- **File size**: Keep under 500KB for optimal loading
- **Quality**: High resolution for lightbox viewing

## Temporary Placeholder

Until real images are added, the application will attempt to load these images. You may see broken image placeholders on the portfolio pages.

To add images quickly, use stock photos from Pexels or Unsplash that represent:
- Security cameras and surveillance equipment
- Network infrastructure and servers
- Professional IT installations
- Educational technology setups
